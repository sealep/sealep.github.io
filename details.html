The AZ48 Half Marathon

The AZ48 half marathon is a flat, fast course that starts and finishes in downtown Chandler. It is USATF-certified this year, which means your PR times will certainly be respected!

The Gilbert Half Marathon

The Gilbert half marathon is a flat course that starts and finishes in Cooley Ranch. It is not USATF-certified. It contains stretches of rocks and dirt, so run carefully!

The Mesa Marathon

The Mesa marathon is Phoenix's flagship marathon, which attracts both recreational and elite runners from across the country. After an initial ascent the first few miles, the majority of the course is downhill, providing an opportunity for a Boston qualifier!
